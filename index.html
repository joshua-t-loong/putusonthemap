#PutWard11OnTheMap

Ward 11 has been left off the mapfor far too long. Overcrowded buses, potholes, empty lots with overgrown weeds: these are but some of the many examples of how our community has been forgotten and ignored.

This neglect also means we're not getting the attention or resources from City Hall that we deserve.

Flavours from every continent, a vibrant and talented arts scene, local businesses and residents associations that care about our community: there are so many things that should be celebrated in Ward 11.

What would you fix? What would you celebrate? Tell us how you would #PutWard11onTheMap!
